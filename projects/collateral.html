Project for 'Sequence & Series'. A collection of photographs for our photography elective.

The project about the immense amount and variety of thoughts that go through our minds every minute, the feelings we create leading up to the explosion of our minds.

Collateral won a student vote for the best photos, with the photos being used as the exhibition poster/invite.

I've made a timelapse from the all the shots I took on the day, take a look behind the scenes!
